Who is this site for?

This site is for individuals and groups passionate about climate action and environmental justice in Boston, including:

Students and Young Activists: Those looking to get involved in local environmental justice organizations, learn about initiatives, and take meaningful action.
Community Members: Residents of Boston and surrounding areas interested in discovering resources, organizations, and tools to support sustainability and climate justice efforts in their communities.
Policy Enthusiasts and Researchers: Individuals researching or supporting policy changes in environmental justice, who could benefit from a comprehensive directory or database of local efforts.
Nonprofits and Volunteers: Organizations or individuals looking to connect with like-minded groups, map their impact, and expand their volunteer networks.
